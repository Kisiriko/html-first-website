Loan Products

Our loan products are designed to support every member’s personal, educational, and business growth.

Types of Loans

  • Emergency Loans – Fast processing for urgent needs
  • Business Loans – Support for small businesses and startups
  • Education Loans – For school fees and tuition support
  • Development Loans – For large projects like building or farming

Loan Requirements:

  • Must be an active member for 3 months
  • Must have sufficient savings
  • Guarantors (for loans above UGX 1M)

Our Saving Plans

Choose from our flexible saving plans tailored to your goals, whether you’re saving solo, for your child, or with a group.

Regular Savings

Save anytime and withdraw whenever needed. This plan is best for day-to-day personal saving with full flexibility.

Fixed Deposit

Lock your funds for a specific time and earn higher interest rates. Ideal for serious long-term goals like construction or education.

Child Savings

Secure your child’s future with an account dedicated to them. Great for saving school fees or teaching financial habits early.

Group Savings

Perfect for community groups or teams. Save together and access benefits like group loans and bonus interest for regular saving.
