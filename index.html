what is e-Fund SACCO

e-Fund is a digital platform that helps SACCOs and other financial groups manage savings, loans, and member records efficiently. It allows users to access services like deposits, withdrawals, and loan applications through mobile or online systems. With features like mobile money integration and automated reports, e-Fund improves transparency, convenience, and financial management for both members and administrators.

We proudly collaborate with leading financial institutions and tech partners across East Africa.
