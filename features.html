Features of e-Fund

At e-Fund SACCO, we are committed to delivering innovative and member-centered financial solutions. Our platform is designed with features that simplify your saving, borrowing, and financial management experience. Whether you're an individual member, group, or organization, our tailored features ensure convenience, transparency, and security every step of the way. Explore the core functionalities that make e-Fund a trusted digital partner in your financial journey.

Member Onboarding (E-KYC)

Digitally register and verify SACCO members using e-KYC tools.

USSD Transactions

Deposit, transfer, and apply for loans via USSD codes.

Loan & Expense Management

Track disbursements, repayments, and expenses in real-time.

Share Capital Tracking

Monitor member share contributions effectively.

Income/Expense Management

Track income and expenses in real-time for transparency.

mCash E-Wallet

Use a digital wallet for secure savings and transactions.

User Role Management

Assign different roles and access rights to users.

Communication Tools

Send SMS, email & in-app updates to members instantly.

Accounting

Maintain digital ledgers for your SACCO's financials.

Reports

Generate detailed system and finance reports easily.
